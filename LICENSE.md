## FewFC-Derived Mini-Case

`data/samples/public_mini_events.jsonl` is derived from the public FewFC research dataset:

- upstream repository: `https://github.com/TimeBurningFish/FewFC`
- upstream license note observed locally: CC BY-SA 4.0

The full raw FewFC dataset is not committed. The converted mini-case retains source metadata and license notes and is used only as a 20-record external sanity case for schema, evidence-span, and replay checks.
